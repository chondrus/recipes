Ingredients:
 - 2 eggplants
 - 1 onion
 - 1 zucchini
 - 1 bell pepper
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - 8 tomatoes
 - 2 tablespoons chili powder
 - 1 teaspoon ground cumin
 - 1 teaspoon ground oregano
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
 - 15-ounce of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
 - 15-ounce of pinto beans, drained and rinsed
 - (optional) 1/2 the pinto beans above, replace with other beans

Directions:
 0. Chop the eggplant, and lightly cover it with kosher salt. Leave it in a collendar over a bowl to drain.
 1. Chop the onion, zucchini, and bell papper, then satuee them lightly. Set aside while you brown the eggplant.
 2. Add all the veggies, chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt, and cayenne into a pot, and water or veggie broth until it covers them.
 3. Stir, then cover and simmer over low heat for one hour.
 5. Add the beans and simmer until they are tender.
